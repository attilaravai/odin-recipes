Lasagna

Description

Pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 2 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of red wine
  • 24 oz Marinara Sauce (3cups)
